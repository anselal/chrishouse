About the Villa

Nestled in a newly built settlement near Nea Moudania, Georgia Villa – Chris House offers a serene retreat just a minute's walk from Portaria Beach. Our modern villa, with its impressive architecture and spacious interiors, provides the perfect setting for relaxation and rejuvenation.

Our Accommodation

Explore our wide range of accommodation options, from spacious apartments to cozy ground-level houses. All units are designed with comfort and elegance.

Our courtyard is a gem, featuring a rustic gazebo, a barbecue, vine-covered shade, and plenty of seating for relaxation. It’s the perfect place to unwind with your loved ones.
