About the Villa

Apartments Tzelepis-Chris House is located on Portaria Beach, in a newly built settlement just a stone's throw from Nea Moudania. Built with insulating materials on the main road, it features a front garden, a swing for children, and small plane trees at the parking. Just a 1-minute walk from the clean sandy beach, it offers guests tranquility and an absolute energy recharge.

At dusk, during sunset when the sea turns red, and a little later when night falls and the moon touches and reflects on its calm surface –yes , this magical dream touches the depths of the visitor’s soul, offering relief, relaxation, and a profound sense of the essence of life.

The architecture of the Villa is modern, impressive, with spacious and open areas where no-one can feel cramped. The facade is double-sided, facing both east and west. The balconies on the second floor are wide, with a stunning panoramic view, where there is always a breeze. The staircase is made of white marble, while the floors and bathrooms are tiled with granite. On this floor, a beautiful and comfortable maisonette is formed, together with the attic. The romantic fireplace is luxurious and beautiful, and the kitchen is fully equipped. There are plenty of single and double beds, while the front doors are armored, thus ensuring security.

On the ground floor, the apartments are very practical, elegant, and conveniently arranged; they are spacious and without stairs, making them ideal for people with mobility difficulties.

Additionally, on the ground floor, there is a residence with a fully equipped kitchen, a bathroom with a shower, a dining area, 2 single beds, and a sofa in the outdoor area.

Accommodation

We offer a variety of accommodation options:

The Ground Floor Apartments offer comfortable accommodation with ample space for relaxation. Each apartment features two double beds, one single bed, and a sofa, while the dining area includes a table with five chairs. Guests can also enjoy the outdoors, with a well-furnished outdoor area equipped with chairs and tables.

The Second-Floor Apartments provide a spacious and cozy environment, ideal for couples. Inside, the apartment is furnished with one double bed, one single bed, and a sofa, while the attic offers an additional two single beds. The fully equipped kitchen and dining table with four chairs make it easy to prepare and enjoy meals. For outdoor relaxation, there are two balconies: the first equipped with a table and four chairs, and the second with a smaller setup of a table and two chairs.

The Ground Floor Studio offers a comfortable and modern living space, ideal for children and the elderly. Inside, the house is furnished with two single beds and features a dining room with a fully equipped kitchen. The outdoor area includes a cozy sofa, table, and chairs, providing a relaxing space to enjoy the fresh air. With its elegant design, including modern white marble and built-in fireplaces, the house combines style and comfort. However, it is not suitable for individuals with wheelchairs or mental disabilities.
