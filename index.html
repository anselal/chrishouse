About the Villa

Villa Georgia-Chris House is located on Portaria Beach, in a newly built settlement just a stone's throw from Nea Moudania. Built with insulating materials on the main road, it features a front garden, a swing for children, and small plane trees at the parking. Just a 1-minute walk from the clean sandy beach, it offers guests tranquility and an absolute energy recharge.

Accommodation

We offer a variety of accommodation options:

Ground Floor Apartments include 2 double beds, 1 single bed, a sofa, and a dining table for 5 with a spacious balcony. The second-floor apartments feature additional attic space and two balconies, while the ground-level house offers 2 single beds, a dining area, and a lovely balcony.

Courtyard & Facilities

The courtyard is paved with stone and tiles, featuring a rustic gazebo, a barbecue, a vine providing thick shade, a bench, chairs, small cypress trees, and many potted plants.

We also offer high-quality amenities such as WLAN, a shared laundry machine, bath towels, air conditioning with a metered charge (0.30€/1kWh), and more.
